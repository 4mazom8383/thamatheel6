About Us

Tamatheel is an event management and consultancy company. We provide an event planning and marketing communication. With experience in a vast range of events, Tamatheel plans and delivers world class events and programs of all sizes in Saudi Arabia.

We will always give the most practical and realistic advice so that we have a clear picture of how the process will work, how we will keep you in the loop, how we’ll mark each milestone and continuously push for high quality from all suppliers and stakeholders.

OUR MISSION


Presenting innovative and global ideas according to the latest international technologies in the creation of distinguished events under the management of our competent forces and our full commitment to quality and professionalism to create unforgettable events.

OUR VISION


To be the first choice in the Kingdom in presenting, creating and innovating distinguished events and exhibitions at all levels.
